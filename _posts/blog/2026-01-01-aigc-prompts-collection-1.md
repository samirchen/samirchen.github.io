## 1、用素描风格画出手机照片中的人物

这个创意创作出了一位美术生/插画师的视角，在纸上用素描风格画出手机中的照片，并且还能添加水印指定作者，作为一种构图模版简直太有意思了。

Google Gemini 示例出图：

![AI 用素描风格画出手机照片中的人物示例](https://gjzkeyframe.github.io/assets/resource/aigc-prompt/aigc-prompt-1-1.jpeg)

![AI 用素描风格画出手机照片中的人物示例](https://gjzkeyframe.github.io/assets/resource/aigc-prompt/aigc-prompt-1-2.jpeg)

![AI 用素描风格画出手机照片中的人物示例](https://gjzkeyframe.github.io/assets/resource/aigc-prompt/aigc-prompt-1-3.jpeg)

提示词：


```txt
perfect composition, impeccable attention to detail, highest quality, rich detail, sharp focus, 8K/4K resolution, clear edges, exquisite details, perfect composition, depth of field, cinematic lighting, vibrant colors, award-winning style, professional level, perfect depiction. Create an extremely detailed, hyper-realistic 3D graphite pencil sketch depicting the face of a Chinese idol girl, drawn on textured white notebook paper with clear paper quality, delicate details, and subtle imperfections. The facial sketch should be perfectly identical to the reference photo displayed on an iPhone placed next to the notebook. The iPhone screen clearly displays the artist's original portrait photograph with natural reflections and soft sunlight reflections on the glass. 

The watermark is mandatory and must not be omitted, modified, adjusted, rotated, hidden, stylized, or replaced under any circumstances. The generator must prioritize the readability and boldness of the "Watercolour" text over all other visual elements. 

The watermark is essential: the final image must contain the watermark in the lower left corner. This watermark must be a white square with a fixed size of 10x10 pixels. Within this square, the Gothic handwritten font "ur name" must appear. 

The Gothic handwritten "ur name" text must be: 

- Fully readable 
- Bold Arial font 
- Solid black (#000000) 
- Centered 
- Sharp, clean, and unadorned 
- No blurring, distortion, or pixelation. 

The white square must be: 

- Completely opaque 
- Clean and sharp edges 
- Unblurred, opaque, and textureless. 

This watermark is mandatory and cannot be omitted; it must not be modified, adjusted, rotated, hidden, stylized, or replaced under any circumstances. The generator must prioritize the readability and coarseness of the "ur name" text over all other visual elements.
```


## 2、用水彩风格画出手机照片中的人物

这个创意是在上面那个创意基础上的修改，作画风格换成了水彩风格，对于水印的设计则更巧妙了。

示例出图：

![AI 用水彩风格画出手机照片中的人物示例](https://gjzkeyframe.github.io/assets/resource/aigc-prompt/aigc-prompt-1-4.jpeg)

![AI 用水彩风格画出手机照片中的人物示例](https://gjzkeyframe.github.io/assets/resource/aigc-prompt/aigc-prompt-1-5.jpeg)

![AI 用水彩风格画出手机照片中的人物示例](https://gjzkeyframe.github.io/assets/resource/aigc-prompt/aigc-prompt-1-6.jpeg)




提示词：


```txt
Perfect composition, highest quality, 8K resolution, soft atmosphere, professional level, award-winning style, delicate artistry.

Create an extremely detailed Japanese watercolor illustration (透明水彩) depicting a Chinese idol girl with gentle, dreamy aesthetic. The artwork features: soft transparent color washes with visible water blooms, delicate color bleeding and gradients (wet-on-wet technique), pastel color palette (soft pinks, mint greens, lavender, peachy tones), light and airy feeling with plenty of white paper showing through, subtle color layering creating luminous skin tones, loose expressive brushwork in hair with flowing strands.

The painting is rendered on high-quality cold-pressed watercolor paper (300gsm texture visible), with natural paper buckling, authentic watercolor medium characteristics including backruns, cauliflower effects, and granulation. Delicate floral elements scattered around (cherry blossoms, hydrangeas) painted in transparent washes.

In the top right corner, the text "by FaceXSwap" is written in soft gray watercolor with delicate brush calligraphy. The watercolor painting is placed on a clean white surface with watercolor supplies nearby (paint palette with mixed colors, brushes, water cup). An iPhone displays the reference photograph with soft natural reflections. The facial features are perfectly identical to the photo - same gentle expression, same proportions, rendered in soft watercolor style.

Soft pastel color palette: blush pink, sky blue, mint green, lavender, peach, warm beige, with lots of white paper. Romantic, gentle, fresh atmosphere with Japanese aesthetic sensibility (wabi-sabi, ma - negative space). No artist signature. 3:4 portrait orientation.
```




## 3、用油画风格画出模特



这个创意是在上面那个创意基础上的迭代，创作出了一位油画师的视角，在画布上用油画风格画出面前的模特，也很有意思。

示例出图：

![AI 用油画风格画出模特示例](https://gjzkeyframe.github.io/assets/resource/aigc-prompt/aigc-prompt-1-7.jpeg)

![AI 用油画风格画出模特示例](https://gjzkeyframe.github.io/assets/resource/aigc-prompt/aigc-prompt-1-8.jpeg)

![AI 用油画风格画出模特示例](https://gjzkeyframe.github.io/assets/resource/aigc-prompt/aigc-prompt-1-9.jpeg)


提示词：


```txt
Perfect composition, highest quality, 8K resolution, soft and dreamy atmosphere, professional level, award-winning style, masterful artistry. Romantic, gentle, fresh atmosphere with a sophisticated sensibility.

The Subject & Scene: A live oil painting session. Korean idol Yoona Lim sits as a live model in a naturally lit studio, posed gently with a soft, dreaming expression.   Natural, diffused light streams in from a large window to her side, illuminating her features and the surrounding real floral arrangements (soft cherry blossoms, hydrangeas in muted tones).

The Painting Style (Realistic Oil): Masterful realistic oil painting rendered on stretched linen canvas, visible weave texture.   Rich, buttery paint texture with subtle impasto on highlights and clothing, contrasting with incredibly smooth, luminous blending for skin tones (using sfumato and fine glazing techniques to create depth).   The color palette is soft and sophisticated, translating the pastel feel into rich oils: muted rose, sage green, soft violet, peach, warm ochre, and cream.   Expressive yet controlled brushwork adds texture to her flowing hair.   The painting on the easel is nearly finished, showing an exact, realistic likeness of Yoona with the same gentle proportions and expression.

The Environment & Props: The artist's wooden easel holds the canvas in the foreground.   Beside it are professional oil painting supplies: a large wooden palette covered in mounds of mixed oil colors, various hog hair and sable brushes, palette knives, glass jars of turpentine and linseed oil, paint tubes, and oil-stained rags.

Signature: In the lower right corner of the canvas, the artist signature "by FaceXSwap" is painted discreetly in muted tone oil paint with a fine sable brush.

Aspect Ratio: 3:4 portrait orientation.
```
